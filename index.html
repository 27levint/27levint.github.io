More Info

Study+ is an interactive studying website to help students do well on their tests. Using two different quiz modes, students can practice in as few as 10 questions. Find tests for your team and teacher. Study+ currently has 6 subjects, Math ELA, Science, Social Studies, Spanish, and French. Students can also make practice quizzes on their own, and submit them. The best quizzes will be adapted into a Study+ quiz for all to see.
